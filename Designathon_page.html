Problem

Lack of convenient means for new students to navigate the campus and a need for a centralized platform to display events organized by the college, college clubs, and the community.
The absence of such a platform makes it challenging for new students to stay informed about events and tasks, leading to difficulty in managing their schedules and staying engaged with the campus community

Next

Solution

Developing a user-friendly app for organizing tasks, prioritizing tasks, and connecting new students by keeping them informed about campus events and happenings.
